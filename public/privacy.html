Privacy Policy

The privacy policy of this service is extremely strict-- no personal information is ever collected, and there is currently no functionality to support personal accounts (meaning there is no worry of having your password or other sensetive information as plain text), this is mainly to uphold the anonymity of our users. When posting a message, you have the ability to select a pseudonym-- choose to use your own name or an easily recognizable pseudonym at your own discretion.

If you would like a message removed or would like to have it altered, visit our contact page and while filling out the form, please include pieces of what the original message contained, and the original pseudonym that you used.

Messages containing incendiary language, offensive rhetoric, and generally asshole-ish vibes will be deleted. Please use your discrection when using this site, and check out the mission statement for a better understanding of what this site is about, and what is considered acceptable.
